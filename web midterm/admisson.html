Admissions Overview

Engineering College welcomes applications from qualified students worldwide who are passionate about engineering and technology. Our admissions process is designed to identify students who demonstrate academic excellence, creative problem-solving abilities, and a commitment to making a positive impact.

We offer multiple intake periods throughout the year and provide flexible study options to accommodate diverse student needs. Our admissions team is dedicated to supporting you through every step of the application process.

Application Deadlines

Fall Intake: June 30 | Spring Intake: December 15 | Summer Intake: April 30

Admission Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent with minimum 75% aggregate
  • Strong performance in Mathematics and Science subjects
  • SAT scores (minimum 1200) or equivalent standardized test
  • English proficiency test (TOEFL 80+ or IELTS 6.5+) for international students
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement explaining your interest in engineering

  • Bachelor's degree in Engineering or related field with minimum 3.0 GPA
  • GRE scores (minimum 310) or equivalent
  • English proficiency test (TOEFL 90+ or IELTS 7.0+) for international students
  • Three letters of recommendation from academic or professional sources
  • Statement of purpose outlining research interests and career goals
  • Resume or CV detailing relevant experience

  • All standard admission requirements apply
  • Valid passport copy
  • Official transcripts with certified English translations
  • Financial statement showing proof of funds
  • Student visa documentation support provided after admission
  • International credential evaluation (WES or equivalent)
