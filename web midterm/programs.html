About Engineering College

Engineering College has been at the forefront of engineering education for over three decades, combining theoretical knowledge with practical application.

Our institution is committed to developing the next generation of engineers, innovators, and leaders. With state-of-the-art facilities, industry partnerships, and a dedicated faculty, we provide students with an education that goes beyond textbooks.

We emphasize hands-on learning, research opportunities, and real-world problem-solving to ensure our graduates are ready to make an immediate impact in their chosen fields.

Computer Science & Engineering

Focus on software development, algorithms, AI, and machine learning with hands-on projects.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Degree: B.Tech
  • Mode: On-campus
Program Overview

Our Computer Science & Engineering program offers comprehensive training in programming, data structures, web development, mobile applications, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity. Students work on real-world projects and internships with leading tech companies.

Career Opportunities: Software Engineer, Data Scientist, AI Specialist, Systems Architect, Cybersecurity Analyst

Key Facilities: Advanced Computing Labs, AI Research Center, Innovation Hub

Electrical & Electronics Engineering

Master power systems, electronics, control systems, and renewable energy technologies.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Degree: B.Tech
  • Mode: On-campus
Program Overview

This program covers circuit design, power systems, microprocessors, control systems, and renewable energy. Students gain expertise in both electrical power engineering and electronics, preparing them for diverse career paths in energy, telecommunications, and automation.

Career Opportunities: Power Systems Engineer, Electronics Design Engineer, Control Systems Specialist, Renewable Energy Consultant

Key Facilities: High Voltage Lab, Power Electronics Lab, Renewable Energy Research Center

Mechanical Engineering

Design, analyze, and manufacture mechanical systems from automotive to aerospace applications.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Degree: B.Tech
  • Mode: On-campus
Program Overview

Study thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, machine design, manufacturing processes, and CAD/CAM. This program emphasizes practical skills through workshops and industry collaborations, covering automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing sectors.

Career Opportunities: Automotive Engineer, Manufacturing Engineer, HVAC Designer, Aerospace Engineer, Product Designer

Key Facilities: CAD/CAM Lab, Manufacturing Workshop, Thermal Engineering Lab, Robotics Lab

Civil Engineering

Build the infrastructure of tomorrow with expertise in structural design and urban planning.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Degree: B.Tech
  • Mode: On-campus
Program Overview

Learn structural analysis, construction management, transportation engineering, geotechnical engineering, and environmental engineering. Students participate in field visits, surveying camps, and construction site projects to gain practical experience.

Career Opportunities: Structural Engineer, Construction Manager, Urban Planner, Transportation Engineer, Environmental Consultant

Key Facilities: Structural Testing Lab, Surveying Lab, Concrete Technology Lab, Environmental Engineering Lab

Master's in Data Science

Advanced analytics, machine learning, and big data processing for data-driven decision making.

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Degree: M.Tech
  • Mode: Online/Hybrid
Program Overview

Master advanced machine learning algorithms, deep learning, big data technologies (Hadoop, Spark), statistical modeling, and data visualization. This program is designed for working professionals with flexible online and weekend classes, featuring industry case studies and capstone projects.

Career Opportunities: Data Scientist, Machine Learning Engineer, Business Intelligence Analyst, Data Architect, AI Research Scientist

Key Facilities: Cloud Computing Lab, Big Data Analytics Center, AI Research Collaboration Platform

Robotics & Automation

Specialized program in autonomous systems, industrial robotics, and intelligent automation.

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Degree: M.Tech
  • Mode: On-campus
Program Overview

Explore robotics kinematics, computer vision, sensor integration, autonomous navigation, and industrial automation. Students work with cutting-edge robotic platforms including drones, manipulator arms, and mobile robots, conducting research in collaboration with industry partners.

Career Opportunities: Robotics Engineer, Automation Specialist, Research Scientist, Industrial Automation Consultant, Autonomous Systems Developer

Key Facilities: Robotics Research Lab, Computer Vision Lab, Industrial Automation Center, Drone Testing Facility
